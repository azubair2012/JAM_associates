Who we are

We are one of Dhaka, Bangladesh's most reputable and sought-after full-service law firms, offering comprehensive expertise and experience across all areas of the law to both businesses and individuals. Our dedicated team is committed to providing unparalleled customer service, tailoring bespoke solutions to address any legal challenges our clients may face. Our firm, along with our individual practitioners, have earned recognition from esteemed legal directories and industry leaders, establishing ourselves as a prominent player in the legal landscape. We take pride in our three offices, including our primary office in Dhaka, a full-service branch in Chittagong, and a specialized family law team located in Sylhet.
Embracing our Bangladeshi heritage, we actively engage with various charitable organizations and fundraising initiatives, giving back to the local community that has shaped our firm's success. Our commitment to excellence, local engagement, and client satisfaction has garnered us numerous awards and accolades for our exceptional work. Whether it's corporate matters, personal legal issues, or any other legal need, we are dedicated to serving our clients with unwavering dedication and proficiency.
